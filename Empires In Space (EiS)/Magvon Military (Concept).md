Magvious is a space nation.

# Magvon Territorial Defence (MTD) (Army of Magvious)

They are responsible for protecting land of planets, moons, asteroids in Magvious and attacking other countries land. They are the army of Magvious.

Let's make the units.

**Squadrons**:
- **A-Squadron**: They are the main attackers. They are at the front lines gaining territory. They are used for invading land or reclaiming land from an enemy country. They can range from 10 soldiers (the bare minimum) to millions, billions, trillions of soldiers.
- **B-Squadron**: They are backups to A-Squadron. They are positioned right behind A-Squadron and the move when A-Squadron moves. If A-Squadron falls, then they take role of being the main front. They also can consist from 10 to trillions of soldiers.
- **C-Squadron**: Basically B-Squadrons. Just right behind them.
- **D-Squadron**: Basically C-Squadrons, just behind them.

Each of the Squadron have a **Squadron General (Placeholder name)**, they are like generals but they are directly in the battlefield, commanding the Squadrons on where and how to attack, they also sometimes attack themselves. Normal soldiers can handle themselves fine and can take orders from the actual general, Squadron Generals are there for more advantage.

**Troopers**:
- **A-Troopers**: They are the main defenders. They defend, protect and secure the occupied territory gained by the Squadrons. They move to the newest occupied territory to defend. They can consist of 10 soldiers (bare minimum) to trillions of soldiers. There are five sub divisions of Troopers:
    - **1st Degree (1°) A-Troopers**: defends, protects and secures the buildings.
    - **2nd Degree (2°) A-Troopers**: defends, protects and secures the neighborhoods.
    - **3rd Degree (3°) A-Troopers**: defends, protects and secures the city/town/village.
    - **4th Degree (4°) A-Troopers**: defends, protects and secures the district or a region in a province/state
    - **5th Degree (5°) A-Troopers**: defends the province or region.
- **B-Troopers**: Basically A-Troopers, just behind them. They have the same sub divisions, just replace A-Troopers with B-Troopers. They move to area where A-Troopers were defending.
- **C-Troopers**: Basically B-Troopers, just behind them. They move to the area where B-Troopers were defending.
- **D-Troopers**: Basically C-Troopers, just behind them. They move to the area where C-Troopers were defending

Each Trooper has a **Trooper General (Placeholder name)** which is just like the Squadron General.

**Guards**:
- **A-Guard**: They are deployed when D-Troopers move out from the occupied territory, leaving it vulnerable to enemy attack. They secure the border of the occupied territory. They are usually deployed when all 3 sides of the occupied territory are surrounded by water bodies; however, they are always deployed when D-Troopers moves out, even if no sides are surrounded by water bodies (they become more important when water bodies aren't surrounding them, as they now have to actively push enemy soldiers).
- **B-Guard**: They are deployed when D-Troopers moves out of an occupied territory. They are like Troopers but more scattered and have less soldiers, typically about a few million. They are meant to stop quick and sudden enemy attacks and not let the area be completely vulnerable. The operate inside the area whereas A-Guards operates at the borders.
---
Types (the types of Squadrons, Troopers and Guards) are named:
- **I-Specialized**: Infantry.
- **M-Specialized**: Armor, Tanks and other armored vehicles.
- **R-Specialized**: Artillery.
- **S-Specialized**: Special Forces.
- **E-Specialized**: Combat Engineers.

They are put before the Squadron, Trooper and Guard name, for example:
- I-Specialized A-Squadron.
- E-Specialized B-Trooper.
- 2° R-Specialized C-Trooper.
- M-Specialized A-Guard.

These are usually shorten to the identifier (the I, M, R, E, S, E in Specialization) being put before the Squadron, Trooper and Guard identifier (A, B, C, D). For example:
- I-A-Squadron.
- E-B-Trooper.
- 2° R-C-Trooper.
- M-A-Guard.

Squadrons, Troopers and Guards are usually mixed with different types specialized Squadrons, Troopers and Guards respectively.
