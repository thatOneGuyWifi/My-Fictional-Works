The × 10 after `max(0, D - (R + 2))` tells how much decay happens. The + 2 in `R + 2` adds radius by 2 because RCEED happens after beyond 2 meters of your ERCE

L = 500 NE

*Example #3 (where R = 0 (no ERCE or for this example, no ERNE))*:
